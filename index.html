Types of Home Insurance Coverage

At SD Life Wealth, we offer a range of home insurance coverage options to ensure comprehensive protection:

  • Building Insurance: Covers the structure of your home against damage from events like fire, storms, and vandalism.
  • Contents Insurance: Protects your personal belongings inside your home from theft, fire, and other risks.
  • Combined Building and Contents Insurance: Provides comprehensive coverage for both your home and its contents.
  • Landlord Insurance: Covers rental properties against damage and loss of rental income.
